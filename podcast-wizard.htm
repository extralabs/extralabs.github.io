Step-by-step podcast creation software for beginners

Podcast Wizard - an innovative application for easy podcast creation!

Creating, editing, and publishing podcasts has never been easier! To make your job as intuitive as possible, we've broken down podcast creation into a few simple steps.

The wizard will help you record music, voice, or other sounds; mix audio; edit and process audio tracks; create web feeds; and upload new podcasts to your web server.

Our application is fully compatible with the RSS 2.0 and iTunes specifications. You can save your podcast in an audio format of your choice, including MP3, OGG, WMA, and WAV. To make it easier for you to upload podcasts to your server, we've included a built-in FTP client.

To develop the latest version of our excellent application, we've used feedback from many users, both experienced podcasters and beginners. Try out Podcast Wizard now, and you'll surely appreciate its amazing power!
