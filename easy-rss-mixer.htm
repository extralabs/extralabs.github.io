Easy RSS Mixer

Easy RSS Mixer is an excellent tool for combining a few web feeds into one feed. You don't need to be an expert on news feeds as it's very easy to use. Just specify the source feeds, and our application will do the job for you!

ERM supports all major feed formats: RSS, Atom, and RDF. To make things easier for you, we've included a number of built-in tools: a scheduler, a keyword filter, a synonymizer, a directory of popular RSS feeds, a preview tool, and an FTP client.
