JPG to PNG

 

Image Assistant lets you easily convert JPEG to PNG. Follow the simple instructions:

1. Download Image Assistant from the official website.

2. Install and launch the application.

3. Select the JPEG files to be converted by clicking the Add Images button.

4. Click the Next button and select PNG as the output format in the drop-down list.

5. Click the Convert button.
 

Besides, Image Assistant provides a number of options to make conversion simpler and more user-friendly. In the batch mode, you can alter the size and resolution of images, rename them, add watermarks or notes, correct colors, as well as rotate or flip images. To make conversion even easier, you can save presets and later load them at your convenience.
