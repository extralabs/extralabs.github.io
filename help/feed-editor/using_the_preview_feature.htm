Using the Preview Feature

Top  Previous  Next

 

 

In Feed Editor, you can always find out how your feed will look in RSS readers even before it is published. To invoke the preview window, press the “Preview Feed” button in the Feed toolbar or select the “Preview Feed…” item of the “Tools” menu or press the “F7” key.

 

 

In the preview window you can test how link works, images are displayed etc. The presence of a usual browser functionality (Go Back, Go Forward, Refresh etc.) will make it much simpler.
