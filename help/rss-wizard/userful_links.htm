Especially for you we have prepared a collection of links that will help a novice to master the RSS and podcasting world.

 

/products.htm - Software for making RSS and image processing software. Free download.

http://blogs.law.harvard.edu/tech/rss - RSS 2.0 specification.

http://www.apple.com/itunes/podcasts/techspecs.html- Apple iTunes specification.

http://www.unclefeed.com/rss/ - Largest RSS feed directory.

http://www.webmasterworld.com/forum98/ - Forum about RSS and related technologies.

http://groups.yahoo.com/group/rss-dev/ - RSS specification working group.

http://groups.yahoo.com/group/podcasters/ - A mailing list for podcasters.

http://www.feedvalidator.org - Validator for RSS feeds. It works with RSS 0.90, 0.91, 0.92, 0.93, 0.94, 1.0, 1.1, and 2.0. It also validates Atom feeds.

http://www.nobodylikesonions.com/feedcheck/ - Validator for Podcast feeds (with iTunes specific namespace tags support).

http://feedshark.brainbliss.com/ - Free tool for submitting your RSS feeds.
